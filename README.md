# Financial-Performance-Product-Metrics-Dashboard-Power-BI
## Project Objective
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze financial performance, product sales trends, and customer segment contributions. Utilized Power Query, DAX, and data modeling to track key KPIs, visualize sales performance, identify top-performing products, and deliver actionable business insights through dynamic reports and filters.

## Dataset Used:
Financial data cleaned.xlsx
https://github.com/Sravanthi338/Financial-Performance-Product-Metrics-Dashboard-Power-BI/blob/main/Financial%20data%20cleaned.xlsx

## Problem Statement
- Businesses often struggle to track sales performance, profitability, and customer segment contributions across multiple products and regions. 
- The objective is to create a centralized dashboard that provides clear visibility into key metrics, sales trends, and business performance to support strategic decision-making.
  
## Process
- Collected and reviewed the sales dataset in Microsoft Excel for initial data understanding and validation.
- Imported the dataset into Power BI for analysis and reporting.
- Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
- Created relationships between tables and built an efficient data model.
- Developed DAX measures for KPI calculations and business metrics.
- Designed interactive visualizations and dashboard layouts to present insights effectively.
- Added slicers and filters for dynamic analysis by year, product, and customer segment.
- Analyzed sales, revenue, profit, and customer segment performance to generate actionable business insights.
  
## Tools Used
Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Microsoft Excel, Data Modeling, Data Visualization.

## Key Insights
- Paseo was the best-selling product and generated the highest sales.
- Sales were highest in 2014, making it the best-performing year.
- The Government and Small Business customer segments contributed the most to sales.
- Some countries performed better than others, showing strong sales opportunities in those markets.
- Monthly revenue changed over time, helping identify sales trends and peak periods.
- The business achieved 11.75M Revenue, 164.62M Profit, and 1.95M Units Sold.
- Product analysis showed that some products were more profitable and sold better than others.
  
## Conclusion
This project demonstrates the use of Power BI to analyze sales and financial data through an interactive dashboard. By applying data cleaning, transformation, data modeling, and DAX calculations, meaningful insights were generated from the dataset. The dashboard helps track key business KPIs, identify top-performing products, understand customer segments, and monitor sales trends, enabling better data-driven decision-making.
